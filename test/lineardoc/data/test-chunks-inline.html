Auri is a Finnish band composed of vocalist and violist Johanna Kurkela, keyboardist and backing vocalist Tuomas Holopainen, and guitarist, keyboardist and pipe player Troy Donockley.[4] Holopainen and Donockley are also members of Finnish band Nightwish.[2] Their self-titled first album, Auri, was released on March 23, 2018.[2][1] and was recorded at Peter Gabriel's Real World Studios.[1]
